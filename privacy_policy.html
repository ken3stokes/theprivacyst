Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

The Privacyst is designed with privacy as its core principle. We prioritize your security and privacy in every aspect of our service.

Data Collection

We operate on a zero-data-collection policy. All operations are performed locally in your browser, and we do not store or transmit any of your data.

How It Works

Our generators operate entirely client-side, meaning:

  • No data leaves your browser
  • No server-side storage
  • No logs or analytics
  • No cookies or tracking

Generated Data

All passwords, SSH keys, usernames, and email addresses are generated using secure algorithms running locally in your browser. We have no access to any generated data.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with or share data with any third-party services.

Updates to Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page. Last updated: November 2024.
